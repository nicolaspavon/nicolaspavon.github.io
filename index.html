Explore My Data Science and Machine Learning Journey

Nicolás Pavón

Hello, I'm a computer science undergraduate student in my final year with a passion for data science and artificial intelligence. With three years of experience as a full-stack web developer using Python Django and React, I've recently delved into the world of data science and machine learning. I'm fascinated by the power of extracting valuable insights from data, and I've been working with tools like RapidMiner and Python's scikit-learn to clean and analyze datasets. I look forward to leveraging these skills to create something amazing with AI in the future.

Data cleaning

My journey in data science has equipped me with essential data cleaning skills, including the understanding of descriptive statistics, data distributions, and techniques for data normalization and standardization.
